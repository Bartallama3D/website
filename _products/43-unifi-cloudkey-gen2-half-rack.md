Introducing our 3D printed Unifi Cloud Key G2 Plus 1U Bracket for 10" racks. This bracket allows you to mount your Cloud Key in a 10" server rack. The brackets takes up only 1U (1.75inches) inches of vertical space in your rack. The bracket is designed to be tight around your cloud key but also has both the top and bottom open to allow for open air cooling of the device.  

\* Cloud Key is not included. Mounting hardware for standard server racks is included. 

We are not affiliated with, collaborating with, or endorsed by Ubiquiti. This product is a 3D Printed, custom designed accessory.
