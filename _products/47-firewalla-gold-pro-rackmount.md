Introducing our heavy duty Firewalla Gold Pro mounting bracket. This bracket allows you to mount your Firewalla Gold Pro in a standard 19 inch server rack. The brackets takes up only 1U (1.75inches) inches of vertical space in your rack. The bracket features 8 keystone slots and comes with 8 keystone blanks + 4 Cat6 Keystones. The power brick holder grips the power brick and holds it in the rear of the unit within the 1U envelope to save space in your rack and avoid dangling wires. This kit includes everything you need to mount your Firewalla Gold Pro in your rack. 

\* Firewalla Gold Pro is not included. Mounting hardware for standard server racks is included!
